General
  1. Layout
    • Singles proceeds through Quads and then Double Elimination.
    • Doubles proceeds through Single Elimination.
  2. Sets
    • Quads - Everyone plays everyone in their quad in a Best of 3. Nobody is eliminated. Stocks and time are halved.
    • Single Elimination - Players are eliminated after one set loss. All sets are Best of 5.
    • Double Elimination - Players are eliminated after two set losses. Sets are Best of 3, except for Top 8, which are Best of 5.
  3. Pick Procedure
    • First stage is decided on by players using any conventional striking procedure or agreement.
    • In subsequent games, the winner bans one stage and the loser picks.
    • A player may not go back to a stage in which that player has already won, unless agreed upon. (only best of 3 sets)
  4. Character Selection
    • Character selection happens after the stage has been decided.
    • You may request a blind pick character selection for game 1 in each set.
    • The winner of the game picks before the loser.
  5. Gameplay
    • Combos past 300% and stalling are not allowed.
    • Intentional usage of serious glitches (ex. character freezing) is disallowed.
    • Disagreements in port priority are determined by RPS.
    • If the game goes to time, it will be tiebroken by stocks, then, percent, then a 1 stock 3 minute game.
    • If the TO objects any gameplay style, be prepared to defend yourself.
    • Sportsmanlike conduct is mandatory - it's Smash; it's a law.
    • Try not to kibitz-commentate ongoing tournament games.
